Replacement vs Repair Options

Your roof is a huge investment and plays an integral part in preserving the condition of your structure. Regular, professional maintenance and repairs will help extend the life of your roof and your building, but everything has a life expectancy, and at some point a full or partial replacement might be your only option.

There are many different factors that can determine the level of service you may need. Things like weathering, material type, age of the roof, build quality, and the existing structure your roof is on, just to name a few. With so many variables to consider, we understand how stressful it can be to determine what is right for your project.

At Englund Roofing, our expert staff will work with you to evaluate the roof's condition and help you make the right decision. Thanks to our team's decades of experience, you can feel confident in knowing you made the correct choice in trusting Englund Roofing to service your existing roof.
