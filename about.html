About Us

Englund Roofing and Sheet Metal

The Twin Cities Roofing Specialists

Since 1994, Englund Roofing & Sheet Metal has been providing expert, quality roofing solutions for commercial buildings, industry, apartment buildings, condomimium associations and business owners throughout the Minnesota Twin Cities of Minneapolis and St. Paul.

Whether your existing flat roof is in need of repair or replacement, or you're looking for a knowledgible contractor to install a flat roof on your new building, you can feel confident choosing Englund Roofing. Whether you are a commercial or industrial business owner, an apartment building manager, or condominium association, Englund Roofing has the roofing solution to fit your needs.

We also replace and install both commercial and residential roofing systems, shingled & flat roofing, and much more. We will work closely with all insurance companies and building owners, resolving all repairs needed. We are dedicated to serving our customers by providing quality craftmanship, materials and service. Englund Roofing makes your decision easier by providing you with answers to all your questions. Give us a call today at 651-917-7663 or send us a question on our Contact Page.
